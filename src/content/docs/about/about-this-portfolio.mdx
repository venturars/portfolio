## About This Portfolio

This portfolio exists as a complement to my main website, **venturarodriguez.xyz**.

While my main website is a more dynamic space where I share ideas, thoughts, games, and ongoing work, this site serves a different purpose. Its goal is to provide a structured and durable collection of selected projects and professional experiences.

Rather than constantly evolving, this portfolio is intended to remain relatively stable over time. It documents work I have done in the past and the experiences that have helped shape the professional I am today. In that sense, it functions more as a curated archive of my technical work and career milestones.

## Why This Format

When building this portfolio, I chose a simple and documentation oriented approach.

The site is built using **Astro**, **Starlight**, **Mermaid**, and **Tailwind**. Since I see this portfolio as documentation of my professional experiences, this stack felt like the right fit, simple, fast, and well suited for structured technical content.

My goal was not to build something visually disruptive, but rather something predictable and easy to navigate, where the focus remains on the content itself. The structure supports this idea by prioritizing clarity, consistency, and readability.

Using Starlight also makes the project easy to maintain. Because I only update this site occasionally, relying on a well defined documentation framework means I don't need to spend time remembering custom patterns or reinventing the structure every time I add new content.

## Project Documentation

For most project experiences, I can't disclose every technical detail due to the nature of the work. However, I have tried to provide enough architectural context and technical information so that engineers with similar experience can understand the types of systems I worked on, the level of complexity involved, the kinds of problems these projects required solving, and the roles and responsibilities I had within each project.

The intention is to offer a clear and honest overview of the work without exposing sensitive details.

## Instructor Experiences

Instructor experiences are documented with a slightly different approach.

Instead of detailing every subject, technology, or class structure, I focus more on the context of the experience, the period in which it occurred, and the learning philosophy I applied when teaching.

In my view, education benefits from flexibility and adaptation to the needs of students and the realities of the market. Even though having a roadmap and essential topics is important, curricula that are too rigid or prescriptive rarely support meaningful learning. Because of that, these sections focus more on the teaching approach rather than exhaustive program descriptions.
